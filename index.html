Cybersecurity Tips & Checklist

Password Security

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account
  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) wherever possible
  • Never share passwords via email or text
  • Use a password manager for secure storage

Email Security

  • Be suspicious of urgent requests for money or information
  • Check sender email addresses carefully
  • Don't click links in suspicious emails
  • Report phishing attempts to your IT department

Device Security

  • Keep software and apps updated
  • Use antivirus software
  • Lock your devices with PIN or biometrics
  • Backup important data regularly

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Protect Your Digital Life

In today's connected world, your personal information, finances, and privacy are constantly at risk from cyber threats. Learning cybersecurity helps you protect what matters most.

Protect Your Family & Friends

Cyber attacks don't just affect individuals - they can spread through social networks, putting your loved ones at risk. Your knowledge can help protect everyone around you.

Advance Your Career

Cybersecurity skills are in high demand across all industries. Whether you work in tech, healthcare, finance, or any other field, understanding security makes you more valuable.

Contribute to a Safer Internet

Every person who learns about cybersecurity makes the internet a safer place for everyone. You become part of the solution, not the problem.
